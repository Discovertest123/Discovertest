Aviation has seen constant innovation since the first airplanes took flight over a century ago, experiencing what experts describe as a series of revolutions. First, ingenious inventors created and tested the first aircraft; second, innovators made strides in safety; third, the market democratized aviation, giving people across the globe access to air travel.
 
A recent article in Forbes describes aviation’s fourth revolution as an ongoing process centered around the issue of sustainability, with a major focus on Sustainable Fuels. As the article notes, Sustainable Aviation Fuels (SAF) could have a major impact on the aviation industry’s carbon footprint, reducing lifecycle CO2 emissions by up to 80%.
 
We live in a world where demand far outpaces supply, a balance that is only going to get more lopsided as we approach 2030.
 
Emerging Fuels Technology (EFT) is focused on providing the technology and processes to produce sustainable aviation fuels efficiently and at scale. EFT is already, as a profitable company, licensing our technology to producers around the globe.
